** Pasta al pesto recipe

*boil a potato and some garlic
*chop up a lot of basil
*smash the potato and garlic
*mix both with basil
*add olive oil
*add some parmigiano
*season with salt and pepper

*boil a big pot of salty water
*add some of the cooking water to the pesto
*cook pasta until al dente
*drain
*incorporate the sauce and the pasta in a hot pan
*enjoy :)
